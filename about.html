About us

What we are and who we are?

Zillion Nerves Innovative Technology. Pvt. Ltd, abbreviated as ZITY, the brand which is going to be a part of everyone’s life. ZITY is an EdTech platform designed and developed to provide unlimited learning experience through interactive and engaging videos both online and offline. Established in 2015 as a home tuition centre with 3 students by a Mother-Son duo in the city of Kollam, Kerala as Jaguar Institute, went up to become an educational service provider to 18 schools in Kerala and Tamil Nadu by 2019, serving more than 27,000 students a year, is now on its way to becoming the market leader in the EdTech industry with Empowerment as its motto.

Why us?

As the COVID-19 Pandemic struck and shook the world, the physical teaching methods shattered leaving a blank academic year, but a blank page to write the beginning of a new era of education. This gave us the idea of creating a powerful platform that can serve both, student and teacher communities. We noticed that though most EdTech platforms claim to have covered all aspects of education through the internet, there are some parts that appear to be untouched. To bridge this gap, we launch ZITY App, an online application to make the lives of teachers and students a whole lot easier. With more than 280 million active student community in India alone, ZITY App will become the inevitable part of their daily life, be it a child or a professional, providing toddler games to enrich brain, to professional and certificate courses to enhance career opportunities. Shortly ZITY App will become a platform where you can…. “Learn Everything Under the Sun”. We believe this idea has the potential to change the way the world looks at education. We intend to include every possible education related parties in our app, including, schools. ZITY App will not only be focusing on academic education but also on skill-based learning. It will pose as an “All in one app for learning”
